Nuestra Historia

The worst-kept secret in Galesburg.

You have to know your way around town to find us — a little converted house on W Berrien St, no flashy sign, flowers out front. That's the whole charm. The family behind the counter has quietly fed the locals, and folks keep coming back the way they always have.

Everything is made the moment you order it. The shells aren't sitting in a warmer — they're a plain tortilla until your ticket comes in, then they hit the fryer. The meat is cooked fresh about every half hour. Same way it's always been done.
